TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: The live dashboard says "Coming Soon" or shows no data?
A: The Render app may be sleeping (free tier). Visit the URL directly
   to wake it up, or refresh the page.

Q: DNS shows "redcrowlabs.com not found"?
A: DNS propagation takes 24-48 hours. Use https://mxtoolbox.com/dnslookupp/
   to check status.

Q: Local testing before deploying?
A: Run: python3 -m http.server 8080
   Then visit: http://localhost:8080/
