Purpose

RedCrowWatch is a civic research and advocacy tool developed by Red Crow Labs. It is designed to document traffic safety patterns at public NYC intersections and present aggregated, anonymized data to the public and city agencies. It is not a law enforcement tool and does not provide evidence admissible in legal proceedings.
